### 2. Queries with constraints
- WHERE clause
```
SELECT column,...
FORM table
WHERE condition
```

Operator | SQL Example
:-: | :-:
BETWEEN ... AND ... | clo_name BETWEEN 0.5 AND 1.5
NOT BETWEEN ... AND ... | clo_name NOT BETWEEN 2000 AND 2010
IN (...) | clo_name IN (1, 3, 5)
NOT IN (...) | col_name NOT IN (1, 3, 5)
